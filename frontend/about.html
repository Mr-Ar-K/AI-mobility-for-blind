AI Mobility Assist

An accessibility‑first, voice‑driven assistant that turns visual scenes into simple, spoken summaries for visually challenged persons.

Voice‑first Multi‑language: English · Telugu · Hindi Keyboard friendly
🎯

Accurate detections

Built on YOLOv8 with domain add‑ons for traffic lights and zebra crossings.

🗣️

Natural voice

Clear, paced speech with adjustable rate and language preferences.

⌨️

Accessible by design

Strong focus outlines, ARIA labels, and robust keyboard flow.

What you can do

Upload & Detect

  • Upload videos for fast object detection.
  • See concise text summaries and play audio.
  • Progress with time left estimates.

Voice Commands

  • Say “go to upload”, “go to detections”, “go to profile”.
  • Smart filtering avoids false triggers from tips.
  • Conversational confirmations and prompts.

Personalized Audio

  • Choose language: English, Telugu, or Hindi.
  • Set your preferred speaking rate.
  • Compact or full voice tips on page load.

Accessible UI

  • Glassmorphism cards and high‑contrast text.
  • Responsive layout and mobile menu.
  • Reduced‑motion support for comfort.

Under the hood

  • FastAPI backend with async endpoints
  • YOLOv8 core model plus traffic lights and zebra crossing custom models
  • gTTS for server‑side audio generation
  • Web Speech API for in‑browser voice recognition and TTS
  • Vanilla JS frontend with progressive enhancement
