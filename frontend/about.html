An accessibility‑first, voice‑driven assistant that turns visual scenes into simple, spoken summaries for visually challenged persons.

🎯

Accurate Detections

Built on YOLOv8 with domain add‑ons for traffic lights and zebra crossings.

🗣️

Natural Voice

Clear, paced speech with adjustable rate and language preferences.

⌨️

Accessible by Design

Strong focus outlines, ARIA labels, and robust keyboard flow.

What You Can Do

📤 Upload & Detect

Upload videos for fast object detection. See concise text summaries and play audio. Progress with time left estimates.

🎙️ Voice Commands

Say "go to upload", "go to detections", "go to profile". Smart filtering avoids false triggers. Conversational confirmations.

🌐 Personalized Audio

Choose language: English, Telugu, or Hindi. Set your preferred speaking rate. Compact or full voice tips on page load.

✨ Accessible UI

Glassmorphism cards and high‑contrast text. Responsive layout and mobile menu. Reduced‑motion support.
